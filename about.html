About unSAME

We're fun, and creative, and sometimes professional!

unSAME is Fun!


We are a group of crazy people from around the world and based around the world! "unSAME" is our slogan and we are dedicated to making unique, stylized, original and mind-blowing games. Since 2019, as a group of students in UCSC, we have been creating various indie games and participating in different game jams and events. We have 50+ games and demos by now!


We really enjoy creating well-polished and highly interactive experiences and our style focuses on game feel —— We are trying to make every touch of the game satisfying and fun. Hope you can see that in our game and enjoy it!
