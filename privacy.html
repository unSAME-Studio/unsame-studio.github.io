Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 25, 2026

This privacy policy describes how unSAME Studio ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with our games and services. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Data Collection

unSAME Studio does not collect, store, or process any personal data or gameplay information from our users. Our games are designed to be played without requiring accounts, logins, or any form of data submission.

Third-Party Services

Our games may be distributed through third-party platforms such as Steam, PlayStation™ Store, Nintendo eShop, and itch.io. These platforms may collect data independently under their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any platform through which you access our games. We do not receive personal data from these platforms.

No Ad Targeting or Data Mining

Our games contain no advertisements, engage in no ad targeting or data mining, and include no analytics or tracking tools. We do not affiliate ourselves with any third parties that engage in such practices.

No AI Training

Your data will never be used to train, fine-tune, or improve any artificial intelligence or machine learning models — by us or any third party. We are firmly committed to this policy and it will not change without explicit, informed consent.

Children's Privacy

Our games do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). Since we do not collect any personal data from any user, no special measures for children's data are necessary. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us so we can address the concern.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store any personal data, there is no personal data for us to provide, modify, or delete. If you have any questions about your rights under applicable data protection laws (including GDPR and CCPA), please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
